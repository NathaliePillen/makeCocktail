Name

Cosmopolitan

Category

Alcoholic Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1/4 oz

    Absolute Vodka

  • 1/4 oz

    Lime juice

  • 1/4 oz

    Cointreau

  • 1/4 oz

    cup Cranberry juice

Instructions

Add all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well and double strain into a large cocktail glass. Garnish with lime wheel.

Glass

Serve: Cocktail glass
